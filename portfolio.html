Investment Themes

Four macro themes shaping the next decade

01
Agentic Shift & Professional Disintermediation
Software has attained the intelligence to interact autonomously with the physical world. Professional roles are being restructured as AI agents take over complex cognitive tasks across law, medicine, finance, and engineering.
02
AI Bioengineering
The convergence of AI and life sciences is producing breakthroughs at a pace impossible five years ago. Drug discovery, protein folding, genomic sequencing, and precision medicine are being radically accelerated.
03
The Intelligence Supply Chain
Every layer of the AI stack — chips, data centers, training infrastructure, deployment tooling — represents a generational investment opportunity. The companies building this foundation will be indispensable for decades.
04
The New Perimeter of Sovereignty
National security, critical infrastructure, and institutional resilience are being rebuilt by software-native companies. Defense tech, autonomous systems, and cybersecurity form a category that is both urgent and durable.

Fund Commitments

Premier funds, typically closed to new investors

Our access to the world's most competitive funds is built on years of co-investments, relationships, and an active presence in Silicon Valley and global innovation ecosystems. We partner with managers who consistently rank in the top decile of venture performance.

Through our Fund of Funds structure, Latin American families and institutions gain exposure to a diversified portfolio of ~600 companies across stages — from Series A to pre-IPO — that would otherwise require direct GP relationships and minimum commitments in the tens of millions.
