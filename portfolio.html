Portfolio

We invest alongside the world's premier venture capital funds — accessing extraordinary companies before they become obvious market leaders.

Built with Conviction

Venture capital returns don't concentrate in a single dimension. Our portfolio is designed to capture them strategically.

01
Vintage & Diversification
Exposure across multiple fund vintages reduces timing risk and smooths the J-curve. No single market cycle captures the full opportunity set of venture capital.
02
Stage
A deliberate mix of early and late-stage funds balances return potential with cash flow profiles. Each stage plays a distinct role in overall portfolio construction.
03
Thematic Focus
Funds are selected based on conviction in their investment thesis — the technologies and industries they back. Thematic alignment ensures the portfolio reflects where transformational value is being created.
04
Manager Profile
The portfolio combines established managers with consistent track records and emerging managers with differentiated access and higher alpha potential. Each carries a distinct risk-return profile.
